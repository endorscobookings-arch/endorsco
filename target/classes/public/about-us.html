Talent Booking Agency was founded in 1999 and has since become one of the top talent bureaus and talent marketing companies in North America. We have assisted thousands of corporations, organizations, charities and other groups in securing expert talent from every industry.

Talent Booking Agency has built great professional relationships with the world's top talent, talent agents and management teams. Our talent booking agency has direct access to over 50,000 of the biggest names from the worlds of business, entertainment, politics, sports and more.

Our team at Talent Booking Agency makes it easy for you to make one of these top talent a part of your next corporate event, virtual meeting, trade show or endorsement campaign. From your initial request, to securing the perfect talent that fits with your budget, to handling all logistics leading up to the event, our agents provide a streamlined booking process that ensures the success of your event.
